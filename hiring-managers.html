How do you know the videos are authentic?

All Videos Are Recorded Natively in the Platform

Candidates cannot upload pre-recorded videos. Everything is recorded live in SoapBoxGen's browser-based platform, which means:

  • No professional editing or post-production
  • No outsourcing to video production services
  • What you see is what you get

Quality Coaching, Not Professional Production

Candidates receive real-time guidance on framing, lighting, and audio‚Äîbut the content is 100% them. Think of it like spell-check for video: it helps them present well, but it's still their authentic self.

You Can Trust What You See

Every video represents how that person actually communicates. If they stumble, restart, or aren't perfectly polished‚Äîthat's real. And it tells you more than a perfectly edited LinkedIn profile ever could.

How do we prevent bias in video screening?

Video can introduce bias‚Äîbut so can resumes. The key is using it responsibly.

Best Practices We Recommend

  • Watch all videos or none. Don't selectively watch based on names, schools, or other resume signals.
  • Use structured evaluation. Rate candidates on specific criteria (communication clarity, relevance of experience, enthusiasm for role).
  • Blind reviews when possible. Have team members watch videos without seeing resumes first to reduce anchoring bias.
  • Focus on content, not presentation style. Evaluate what they say, not how they look or sound.

What We DON'T Do

  • No AI personality analysis or emotion detection
  • No algorithmic scoring based on facial features or voice patterns
  • No ranking or filtering based on video attributes

You're in control. Video is input, not a decision-making algorithm.
